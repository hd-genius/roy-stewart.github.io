Mathematics Cheat Sheet

Exponents

Product

ax × ay = ax + y

Quotient

ax ÷ ay = ax - y

Power

axy = ax × y

Logarithms

Product

log(a×b) = log(a) + log(b)

Quotient

log(a÷b) = log(a) - log(b)

Power

log((ax)y = y × log(ax)
